Welcome to Tau Ya Barita

Tau Ya Barita Guesthouse is a modern, state-of-the-art accommodation located in Marobala Village, Ga-Dikgale, Polokwane. The facility offers 10 elegantly furnished rooms, each equipped with a flat-screen TV, DStv, and bar fridge, combining village tranquility with urban sophistication.

Guests enjoy secure, ample parking and a relaxing atmosphere ideal for both business and leisure stays. Tau Ya Barita Guesthouse redefines village hospitality with a touch of modern luxury and professional comfort.
